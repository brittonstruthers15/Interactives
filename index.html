Trump wants Greenland, Canada and the Panama Canal.

By Britton Struthers-Lugo

Source: Doug Mills/The New York Times

President Elect Donald Trump says he wishes to purchase Greenland from Denmark, acquire Canada as the 51st state and control the Panama Canal.

According to data from Ballotpedia, the United States' last land acquisition was the Marshall Islands in 1947 via cession. The last land purchase was more than a century ago, when the U.S. bought the Virgin Islands from Denmark for $25 million. Below is a list of the United States' historical land acquisitions via purchase, starting with the most recent.

  • Virgin Islands, 1917
  • Philippine Islands, 1898
  • Alaska, 1867
  • Gadsgen Purchase, 1853
  • Mexican Cession, 1848
  • Florida Purchase, 1819
  • Louisiana Purchase, 1803

While Donald Trump said that he would rely on economic coercion to acquire the land, he also said that he would not rule out using force to acquire Greenland and the Panama Canal.
